The three layers of Physical AI

Every intelligent robot runs on this stack — from physical hardware to motor control to high-level reasoning.

Hardware & Actuators

The physical robot body — motors, joints, sensors, and end effectors that interact with the real world. Companies like FANUC, Yaskawa, and Boston Dynamics build this layer.

Sensorimotor Policy

The motor control system that translates high-level reasoning into precise physical movement. Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models handle locomotion, balance, and manipulation execution.

Foundation Models

The reasoning layer — task planning, understanding context, deciding what to do and why. This layer requires massive real-world post-training data to learn industrial skills.

PhysicAI provides the industrial post-training data that trains the Foundation Model layer — the most data-hungry part of the stack.
