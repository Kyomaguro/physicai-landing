2018 — TOKYO, JAPAN

The Painless Needle
That Died With Its Maker

Okano Industries invented the world's thinnest needle — painless injection technology that could have changed medicine globally. The company was profitable. The product was loved. But when the founder retired, the business shut down.

The reason? The micro-precision welding technique that made the needle possible lived entirely in one man's hands. No documentation. No data. No successor.

「技術は人の手の中にあった。記録されることなく、消えた。」
